About

I'm a senior journalism student at the University of Missouri. I have spent my past two years at the convergence newsroom as a multimedia journalist working for KBIA, Missouri Business Alert and Columbia Missourian.

I have always been curious about new things that are happening around and wanting to discover the stories behind them. I love talking to people and learning from different insights. My curiosity has allowed me to witness the power and importance of voices from people and communities. Now, I want to share that with the public.

I believe in the objectivity and significance of journalism. With the passion for new technologies and emerging media, I would like to use my multimedia skills to make news easily accessible and digestible to people who care about facts and want to bring good causes to the society.
